Understand Our Privacy Practices

1. Information We Collect

When you use Quick Snap, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide during registration or profile updates.
  • Event and Photo Data: Photos, videos, and related metadata (such as location, tags, and timestamps) that you upload or capture using the app.
  • Usage Information: Data related to how you use the app, including features accessed, interactions with other users, and preferences.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access Quick Snap, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to enhance your experience with Quick Snap and to provide our services effectively. Specifically, we may use your data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account, including authentication and profile management.
  • To allow you to capture, store, and organize photos and videos based on events.
  • To provide personalized features such as face recognition and automatic photo tagging.

3. Sharing Your Information

Quick Snap does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as described below:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you provide your explicit consent.
  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the app, such as cloud storage providers, analytics services, and customer support.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our legal rights and the safety of others.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it. This includes encryption, secure data transmission, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can do this by logging into your account and updating your profile settings. If you need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@quicksnap.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of this page and providing a notice within the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
